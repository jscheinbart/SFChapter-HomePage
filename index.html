San Francisco Codecademy Chapter

Mission Statement

This chapter, founded on February 17th, 2021 exists to provide a space for those pursuing further education in the code realm. The mission of this chapter is to foster a community of support and networking, for the purposes of sharing, giving and receiving feedback, networking, and learning from industry professionals.

About Us

This chapter exists to serve the students of Codecademy. We are based in San Francisco and host bi-monthly events to further the educational and professional goals of Codecademy students.
